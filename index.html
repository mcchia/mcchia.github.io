Agile DeliveryProduct Ops

Reducing Feature Lead Time by 37.5%

The team was delivering features in 4 weeks on average — too slow for the product's growth stage. I diagnosed the root causes: unclear ownership, excessive context switching, and stakeholder bottlenecks. By redesigning workstreams and sprint processes, I brought lead time down to 2.5 weeks.

Key Changes Made
Introduced developer-owned workstreams with clear ticket ownership
Formalized sprint retrospectives to capture and action blockers
Reduced stakeholder bottlenecks by async-first decision protocols
Centralized task monitoring in ClickUp for full visibility

Product StrategyWeb3

Sunsetting ERC721Ex with Product Judgment

Not every product should be maintained forever. ERC721Ex had declining market relevance, rising maintenance costs, and diminishing user engagement. I worked with C-suite to evaluate the full cost-value picture and make the strategic call to sunset — freeing team resources for higher-value work.

Why this matters
Product judgment isn't only about what to build — it's about what not to maintain. This decision protected team focus and freed resources for Mintology's growth phase.
