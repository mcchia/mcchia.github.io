Product Ops: 37.5% Faster Feature Lead Time

Reduced average feature delivery from 4 weeks to 2.5 weeks by improving ownership clarity, sprint structure, and stakeholder alignment.

Product OpsAgile DeliveryClickUpSprint Planning
37.5% faster lead time
Situation

Feature delivery was slowed by unclear ownership, stakeholder alignment gaps, and limited visibility across workstreams. The bottleneck was not engineering execution — it was decision latency and context-switching between teams.

What I Did

Average feature lead time dropped from 4 weeks to 2.5 weeks — a 37.5% improvement. Customers received fixes and features sooner; the support team gained predictable release windows to communicate to users.
