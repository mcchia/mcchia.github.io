Delivery Operations: 37.5% Faster

Cut average feature delivery time from 4 weeks to 2.5 weeks by fixing the real bottleneck: not engineering capacity, but ownership gaps and late stakeholder decisions.

Delivery OperationsCross-Team CoordinationProcess ImprovementStakeholder Management
37.5% faster lead time
Challenge

Features were consistently late — not because of engineering, but because of unclear ownership, slow stakeholder decisions, and no shared visibility across teams. The same blockers surfaced every cycle.

Actions
Centralised delivery tracking with clear team ownership for every active workstream
Introduced retrospectives to identify and eliminate recurring blockers
Moved stakeholder decisions to async communication to remove bottlenecks from the critical path
Streamlined QA handoffs and release sign-off to prevent late-stage delays
Results
Feature delivery dropped from 4 weeks to 2.5 weeks (37.5% faster)
Team throughput improved 20% with no additional headcount
Customer support gained predictable release windows to set expectations with users
