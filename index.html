Workflow Transformation: Reducing Feature Lead Time by 37.5%

Context: At Mintable & Mintology, feature delivery cycles were slow and inconsistent, with unclear ownership and recurring stakeholder meetings consuming operational time.

Process ImprovementStakeholder Alignment
37.5% faster lead time
Problem

Features regularly took 4 weeks from requirement to release, with ownership gaps and repeated status meetings creating delay and rework.

Actions
Mapped the delivery workflow end-to-end and clarified ownership at each stage
Introduced dependency tracking across teams
Moved recurring status updates to async dashboards and reporting
Result
Feature lead time: 4 weeks → 2.5 weeks (37.5%)
Team throughput up 20%
Recurring meeting time down 30%
Skills Demonstrated

Workflow mapping, process improvement, stakeholder communication, operating rhythm design.
